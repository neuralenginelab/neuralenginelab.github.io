# Neural Engine

### Mapping the Landscape of Brain-Inspired Computing

An independent research archive exploring the foundations of computational neuroscience, neuromorphic engineering, memristive systems, and brain-inspired intelligence.

</div>

---

## About

Neural Engine is a curated knowledge repository dedicated to preserving, organizing, and connecting influential research across the fields that inspired modern intelligent systems.

From biological neurons and synaptic plasticity to memristive devices and neuromorphic processors, the archive traces more than a century of ideas that continue to shape the future of computing.

---

## Domains

- Foundations of Neural Computation
- Neuron Models & Computational Neuroscience
- Synaptic Plasticity & Learning Rules
- Neural Cultures & Multi-Electrode Arrays
- Network Dynamics & Collective Behavior
- Memristors & Memristive Systems
- RRAM & Emerging Memory Technologies
- Neuromorphic Hardware Architectures
- Connectomics & Brain Mapping
- Cortex Physiology & Biological Intelligence
